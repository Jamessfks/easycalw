AI assistant for students to stay organized and never miss a deadline. Setup takes ~30 minutes, no coding required. Works via WhatsApp, Telegram, or Discord.

## Pain Points Addressed
- Missing assignment deadlines
- Can't keep track of multiple classes
- Forgetting lecture content
- Procrastination and overwhelm
- Study time that doesn't stick
- No balance between school and life

## Features
- Assignment reminders with appropriate lead time
- Note organization — dump notes via text/photo/file, ask questions later
- 24/7 concept explanations
- Study techniques: spaced repetition, active recall, timed sessions
- Break big projects into manageable steps
- Balance tracking for school + life

## Real Use Cases
1. **Assignment Tracker**: Log assignments, get reminders, track progress
2. **Study Session Support**: AI generates practice questions from notes, spaced repetition
3. **Note Organization**: Send notes, search by class/date/topic, generate study guides
4. **Exam Preparation**: Compile materials, generate practice questions, identify gaps
5. **Reading Comprehension Partner**: Log readings, answer comprehension questions, connect to lectures

## Day in the Life Example
- 7:30 AM: Morning check with today's schedule and deadlines
- 10:00 AM: Quick voice note after lecture, AI files it
- 2:00 PM: Quiz on last week's concepts before study group
- 5:00 PM: AI helps break essay into steps
- 9:00 PM: AI suggests taking a break after 4 hours
- 10:30 PM: Quick deadline check

## FAQ
- Not cheating — helps organize/study, doesn't do the work
- Won't write papers — brainstorming/outlining help only
- Privacy — runs on infrastructure you control
- Different from ChatGPT — knows your specific courses, remembers notes, tracks deadlines
