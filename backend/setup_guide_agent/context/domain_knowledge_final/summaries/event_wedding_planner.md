### 37. AI Wedding Planning Assistant

- **Industry:** Event Planning / Personal Event
- **Target Persona:** Couples who are in the midst of planning their wedding and feel overwhelmed by the numerous administrative tasks involved, such as vendor coordination and managing guest RSVPs. These users typically have moderate technical comfort and are looking for a way to automate routine communications and tracking to reduce stress and save time.
- **The Execution Story:** A couple, frustrated by the complexity and volume of wedding planning tasks, developed an OpenClaw-powered assistant named "Paige" to streamline their workflow. The system automatically tracks vendor contracts, ensuring deadlines and payment schedules are met without manual oversight. It maintains an up-to-date guest list with RSVP statuses, sending polite but firm follow-up emails to guests who have not responded by the deadline. The AI-generated emails are drafted for review to maintain a warm and personal tone appropriate for family and friends. This automation significantly reduced the couple’s administrative workload, allowing them to focus on the creative and celebratory aspects of their wedding.
- **Tools & Integrations Mentioned:** OpenClaw, Email provider (for sending and receiving RSVP communications), Spreadsheet integration (for managing guest lists and vendor details)
- **Setup Notes & Warnings:** Intermediate level setup requiring some familiarity with automation workflows and email integration. The most challenging part is configuring the AI to generate communications that feel empathetic and personal rather than overly formal or robotic, which is critical given the emotional nature of wedding planning. It is strongly advised to review all automated messages before sending, especially those directed to close family members, to avoid misunderstandings or offence. There may be minor costs associated with email service usage and spreadsheet software, and users should ensure data privacy when handling personal guest information.
