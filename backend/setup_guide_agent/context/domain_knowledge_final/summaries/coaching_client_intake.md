### 25. Automated Client Intake for Coaches

- **Industry:** Coaching / Consulting
- **Target Persona:** Independent coaches or consultants who are seeking to streamline and automate the often time-consuming process of scheduling initial discovery calls and collecting important client information beforehand. These professionals typically have moderate technical comfort and are looking for a solution that reduces administrative overhead so they can focus more on client engagement and less on logistics.
- **The Execution Story:** A coaching business implemented OpenClaw to fully automate their client intake workflow, significantly reducing manual effort and response times. When a prospective client reaches out, the AI agent immediately responds to initial inquiries and sends a customised pre-session questionnaire to gather relevant background information. Once the questionnaire is completed, the agent automatically accesses the coach’s calendar through a two-way integration to schedule the first discovery call at an available time slot. Prior to the session, OpenClaw compiles the questionnaire responses into a concise prep brief, which is delivered to the coach to ensure they are fully prepared. This automation reduces scheduling friction, improves client experience, and saves several hours of administrative work each week.
- **Tools & Integrations Mentioned:** OpenClaw AI agent, Online form builder for questionnaires, Calendar integration with Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook
- **Setup Notes & Warnings:** Intermediate level setup is required, particularly to configure the calendar integration correctly. The most challenging aspect is ensuring real-time, two-way syncing between the AI agent and the calendar system to prevent double bookings or scheduling conflicts. It is critical to thoroughly test the scheduling workflow before going live to avoid client frustration. Additionally, coaches should be aware of potential privacy considerations when handling sensitive client data through automated forms and ensure compliance with relevant data protection regulations.
