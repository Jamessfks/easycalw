# Competitive Landscape

Our market maps to three distinct service layers:

**Layer 1 — Setup:** Model deployment to the cloud. Get AI agents running on infrastructure. Managed hosting, one-click deploys, cloud provisioning. (24+ providers mapped in setup-layer.md)

**Layer 2 — Upgrade:** Setup + consulting on how the agent should be configured for the customer's specific workflow. Goes beyond deployment into the logic and personality of the agent. (34 companies across 8 tiers in upgrade-layer.md)

**Layer 3 — Observability:** Ongoing integration, monitoring, and maintenance of AI agents within customer workflows. Long-term optimization, not just initial setup. ($1.4B market today, $10.7B by 2033. Analysis in observability-layer.md)

## Where EasyClaw sits

EasyClaw occupies the **upper-right quadrant**: high personalization at affordable scale. Nobody else automates the discovery/interview step at this price point ($200-$2K range is nearly empty).
