### 26. Automated Client Onboarding for Consultants

- **Industry:** Professional Services
- **Target Persona:** Consultants or agency owners who frequently onboard new clients and find themselves overwhelmed by repetitive administrative tasks such as creating digital folders, sending out welcome packets, and scheduling initial meetings. These individuals often have limited time and seek a reliable automation solution that can streamline onboarding without requiring advanced technical skills.
- **The Execution Story:** This consulting business leverages OpenClaw to transform their client onboarding process, cutting down what used to take several hours to just fifteen minutes. The workflow begins when a new client is added to the system or a trigger such as a signed contract is detected. OpenClaw then automatically creates a structured set of folders in Google Drive tailored to the client’s project needs. Simultaneously, it drafts and sends a personalised welcome email containing all necessary forms and instructions, then monitors for the return of signed documents. Once the paperwork is complete, the agent integrates with the calendar system to schedule a kickoff call at a mutually convenient time. This seamless orchestration reduces manual errors, improves client experience, and frees up valuable consultant time.
- **Tools & Integrations Mentioned:** OpenClaw, Google Drive for document management, Email provider for sending and receiving communications, Calendar integration for scheduling meetings
- **Setup Notes & Warnings:** Setup requires an intermediate level of technical knowledge, particularly in configuring API connections and workflow triggers. It is strongly recommended to initially run this onboarding workflow in draft mode to allow manual review of AI-generated emails and folder structures. Monitoring the process closely for several weeks helps identify and correct any errors or miscommunications before fully automating the system. Additionally, consultants should be aware of potential security considerations when automating client data handling and ensure compliance with data protection regulations.
