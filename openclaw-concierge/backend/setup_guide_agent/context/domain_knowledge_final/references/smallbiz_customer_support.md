Key sections relevant to this use case (small business customer support):

- **Automate Customer Support Responses:** OpenClaw manages up to 80% of routine questions by processing uploaded product manuals, FAQs, and documentation. Provides instant answers about pricing, business hours, shipping, and services through WhatsApp, Telegram, or email. Customize responses by defining business rules and tone in SOUL.md.

- **Create Shared Lists and Reminders:** AI creates collaborative task lists, project checklists, or inventory trackers with real-time updates and reminders.

- **Security:** Pairing-first authentication model, loopback-only binding, Docker-based sandboxing, AES-256-GCM encryption for credentials.

- **Cost:** $10–$40 monthly in AI compute fees. Saves 10–15 hours per week.
