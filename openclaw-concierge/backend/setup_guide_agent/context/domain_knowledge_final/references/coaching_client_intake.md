The average coach spends 40% of their week on non-billable work. Intake forms, scheduling back-and-forth, session prep, follow-up emails. That's two full days a week you're not getting paid for.

## Common Pain Points

### Prospects ghost you after the first inquiry
Someone fills out your contact form at 9pm on a Tuesday. By the time you respond Thursday, they've already booked a discovery call with another coach. The coaches who respond within an hour close 3x more discovery calls than those who wait a day.

### Session prep eats into your billable hours
15-20 minutes per client reviewing notes, checking homework commitments, pulling frameworks. Multiply by 20 clients = an entire workday lost each week.

### Follow-up falls through the cracks
Post-engagement follow-up drives referrals but gets deprioritized when running back-to-back coaching calls.

### You're the bottleneck in your own business
Invoicing, proposals, contract renewals, content creation. Capacity tops out at 15-20 clients not because of session time, but because of everything around it.

## What OpenClaw Does for Your Practice

### Instant Client Intake
A prospect fills out your form or sends an email. OpenClaw responds in seconds, asks about their goals, current challenges, and timeline. Then it books them directly on your calendar and sends you a pre-call brief.

### Session Prep Briefs
Before each session, OpenClaw pulls together a one-page brief: what you covered last time, commitments the client made, progress on their goals, and suggested talking points. Delivered to your inbox 30 minutes before the call starts.

### Between-Session Follow-Up
Automated mid-week check-ins, accountability nudges, and resource sharing. OpenClaw drafts personalized messages based on what each client is working on. You review before they send.

### Referral & Re-Engagement
When an engagement wraps up, OpenClaw keeps the relationship warm. Quarterly check-ins, relevant article shares, anniversary touchpoints.

### Proposals & Invoicing
Tell OpenClaw about a new engagement and it drafts the proposal from your templates, tracks contract status, sends payment reminders, and flags overdue invoices.

### Content & Thought Leadership
OpenClaw turns your session insights (with client details removed) into LinkedIn posts, newsletter drafts, and blog outlines.
