## OpenClaw for Dental Practices

OpenClaw sends appointment reminders, runs recall campaigns, welcomes new patients, requests reviews, and manages your waitlist. All automatically. All for about $25/month.

**Key Stats:**
- $200+ average cost per no-show appointment slot
- 10+ hours/week saved on patient communication
- ~$25/month total cost vs dedicated front desk staff

## The Problem

Between checking patients in, answering phones, verifying insurance, and handling billing, proactive patient outreach gets dropped first. That's where revenue leaks start.
- No-shows cost $200+ per empty chair ($50K-$100K/year lost)
- Recall campaigns run once then die
- New patient intake is still clipboard-and-pen
- Happy patients walk out without leaving reviews

## 8 Automations

1. **Smart Appointment Reminders** (saves 3 hrs/week) — Text/email at 72hr, 24hr, 2hr before. Patients confirm/reschedule/cancel with one tap. Cancellations trigger waitlist fill.
2. **Recall Campaign Automation** (saves 4 hrs/week) — Personalized outreach for overdue hygiene/checkup. Text at 6 months, email at 6.5 months, phone queue at 7 months. Adapts based on response.
3. **New Patient Welcome Sequence** (saves 2 hrs/week) — Welcome email, digital intake forms link, insurance verification instructions, "what to expect" guide. All within minutes.
4. **Insurance Verification Follow-Up** (saves 2 hrs/week) — Flags incomplete/missing insurance details, sends follow-up request before appointment.
5. **Post-Visit Review Request** (saves 1 hr/week) — Review request text 2 hours after appointment with direct Google Business Profile link.
6. **Treatment Plan Follow-Up** (saves 2 hrs/week) — Follow-up at 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks with procedure info, financing options, booking link.
7. **Waitlist Management** (saves 2 hrs/week) — Cancellation triggers immediate texts to waitlist patients. First come, first served.
8. **Birthday and Milestone Outreach** (saves 1 hr/week) — Personalized birthday messages, anniversary messages, special offers.

## Patient Nurturing Journey
1. New Patient Inquiry → 2. Instant Response (within 60 seconds) → 3. Qualification and Scheduling → 4. Pre-Visit Preparation → 5. Post-Visit Follow-Up → 6. Ongoing Recare Nurturing
